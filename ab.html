About AG Enterprises

Leading the solar revolution with innovative solutions

Our Company

If you’re sick of high electric bills or have a strong desire to help the environment while still getting the energy you need, solar panels are the solution that you’ve been looking for. Purchasing your own solar panel array and having a grid-tiered solar power system installed is an extremely cost effective and responsible energy consumption choice.

AG Enterprises is a premier solar energy solutions provider dedicated to delivering high-quality, affordable solar power systems for residential and commercial properties. Founded in 2020, we've grown from a small local installer to one of the region's most trusted solar companies.

Our team of certified professionals has installed over 450+ solar systems, helping our customers reduce their carbon footprint while saving money on energy costs.
